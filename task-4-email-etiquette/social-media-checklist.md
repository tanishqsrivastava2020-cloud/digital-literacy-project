# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

## ✅ Do’s

* Maintain a professional and respectful tone in posts
* Verify information before sharing
* Use privacy settings to protect personal data
* Share educational and meaningful content
* Think before posting anything online

## ❌ Don’ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not engage in online arguments or harassment
* Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news
* Do not misuse someone else’s content without permission
