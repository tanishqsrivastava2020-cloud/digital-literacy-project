# Stay Safe Online – Advanced Prevention Checklist

## Awareness & Behavior

* Always question urgency in calls/messages claiming financial risk
* Do not trust unknown callers, even if they appear professional
* Never share personal or financial information without verification
* Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true

## Account & Device Security

* Use strong, unique passwords for each platform
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all critical accounts
* Regularly update apps and device software for security patches
* Install apps only from official sources like Google Play Store

## UPI & Financial Safety (HIGH PRIORITY)

* Remember: You receive money without entering a PIN; entering a PIN means you are paying
* Never approve unknown or unexpected collect requests
* Always verify the sender before accepting any payment request
* Avoid scanning unknown QR codes

## Safe Internet Practices

* Avoid using public WiFi for banking or transactions
* Check website URLs carefully before entering sensitive information
* Do not click on suspicious or shortened links

## Reporting & Immediate Action

* Immediately report fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24×7 in India)
* Inform your bank immediately to block further transactions
